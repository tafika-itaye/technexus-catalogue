1. Who We Are

TechNexus ("we", "us", "our") is a business registered in the Republic of Malawi (BRN: A6SNWQY). We supply ICT hardware, custom PC assembly, software development services, language and translation services, and medical equipment to businesses, government entities, and development partners across Southern and East Africa.

This Privacy Policy explains how TechNexus collects, uses, stores, and protects personal data when you visit www.technexusmw.com or submit an enquiry to us.

2. What Data We Collect

We collect only the minimum data necessary to respond to your enquiry or deliver our services:

  • Enquiry form data: Name, organisation name, email address or WhatsApp number, service selected, and message content — submitted voluntarily via our contact forms.
  • Language Services data: For translation, transcription, or interpretation projects, we may handle source documents that contain personal or sensitive information belonging to your organisation or its beneficiaries.
  • Analytics data: Anonymous usage data via Google Analytics (GA4) with IP anonymisation enabled. This includes pages visited, session duration, and device type. No personally identifiable information is sent to Google Analytics.
  • Technical data: Standard server/CDN access logs (IP address, browser type, referring URL, timestamp). These are retained by our infrastructure provider (GitHub Pages / Cloudflare) and are not collected or stored by TechNexus directly.

We do not use cookies for tracking, advertising, or profiling. We do not run any advertising networks or retargeting systems.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To respond to your enquiry via WhatsApp or email.
  • To prepare quotations, tender submissions, or service proposals you have requested.
  • To deliver contracted language services, including translation and transcription.
  • To understand aggregate site usage via anonymised analytics and improve our website.
  • To comply with legal obligations under Malawian law.

We will never use your data for unsolicited marketing, sell it to third parties, or use it for automated profiling or decision-making.

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Union, European Economic Area, or United Kingdom, we process your personal data on the following legal bases under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f)): Responding to business enquiries you have initiated.
  • Contract performance (Art. 6(1)(b)): Processing data necessary to deliver services you have contracted with us.
  • Legal obligation (Art. 6(1)(c)): Compliance with applicable Malawian financial and tax law.
  • Consent (Art. 6(1)(a)): For analytics cookies, where consent has been obtained.

5. Cookies and Analytics

This website uses Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to collect anonymised usage statistics. We have configured GA4 with IP anonymisation (anonymize_ip: true). No advertising features or remarketing audiences are enabled.

The analytics cookie set by Google Analytics (_ga, _ga_*) is classified as a functional/analytics cookie. By continuing to use this website you consent to this limited, anonymised analytics use. You may opt out at any time by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

We set no other cookies. We do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or fingerprinting.

6. Language Services and HIPAA-Aware Handling

Our TechNexus Scripts division provides translation, transcription, subtitling, and interpretation services. Some projects may involve documents or recordings that contain protected health information (PHI), patient records, legal documents, or other sensitive personal data on behalf of healthcare clients.

For such projects we apply the following safeguards:

  • Source materials are handled only by the assigned language professional(s) bound by confidentiality.
  • Files are transferred via secure, encrypted channels (not unencrypted email attachments).
  • Completed deliverables and source materials are deleted from our working systems within 30 days of project completion unless the client requests otherwise in writing.
  • We do not retain, copy, or sub-license client content for any purpose beyond the commissioned project.
  • Clients in regulated industries (healthcare, legal, government) are encouraged to execute a Data Processing Agreement (DPA) with TechNexus before project commencement. Contact us to request a DPA.

Note: TechNexus is based in Malawi and is not a US-based Covered Entity or Business Associate under HIPAA. Our "HIPAA-aware" designation means we apply equivalent data handling standards as a matter of professional practice and client expectation.

7. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We share personal data with the following third parties only, and only to the extent necessary:

  • WhatsApp (Meta Platforms): Enquiry form submissions are sent to our business WhatsApp number. Your message data is processed under WhatsApp's own privacy policy. You initiate this transfer voluntarily by clicking "Send via WhatsApp".
  • Google Analytics (Google LLC): Anonymised usage data as described in Section 5.
  • GitHub / Cloudflare: Our website infrastructure providers. They may process standard access logs. We do not pass enquiry data to them.

We do not share personal data with any other third parties, advertisers, or data brokers.

8. Data Retention

  • Enquiry data: Retained in our WhatsApp business account for as long as commercially necessary, typically up to 2 years, then deleted.
  • Language Services project files: Deleted within 30 days of project completion unless otherwise agreed in writing.
  • Analytics data: Google Analytics data retention is set to 14 months.
  • Infrastructure logs: Managed by GitHub/Cloudflare per their own retention policies (typically 30 days).

9. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten"): Request deletion of your data where no legal obligation requires retention.
  • Right to restrict processing: Request that we limit how we use your data.
  • Right to data portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Right to object: Object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Right to withdraw consent: Where processing is based on consent, withdraw it at any time without affecting prior processing.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details in Section 14. We will respond within 30 days. We do not charge a fee for reasonable requests.

10. International Transfers

TechNexus operates from Malawi. Data submitted via our enquiry forms is received in Malawi and processed there. When you use Google Analytics or WhatsApp, your data may be transferred to servers in the United States or other countries. These transfers are governed by the privacy policies and standard contractual mechanisms of Google and Meta respectively.

11. Security

We implement the following technical security measures on this website:

  • HTTPS enforced for all connections with HSTS (HTTP Strict Transport Security) preloading.
  • Content Security Policy (CSP) headers to mitigate cross-site scripting risks.
  • X-Frame-Options set to DENY to prevent clickjacking.
  • No server-side database — this website is a static site with no back-end data store.
  • Email addresses obfuscated against automated scraping.

No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but maintain commercially reasonable safeguards.

12. Children's Privacy

Our website and services are directed exclusively at businesses and professionals. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under the age of 18. If you believe a minor has submitted data to us, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of the website after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
